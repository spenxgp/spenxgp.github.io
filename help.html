HELPFUL NOTES

  • Decepción: means disappointment not deception
  • Argumento: this word is used for legal arguments not for an argument your having with someone
  • Chocar: used to refer to a state of shock or a car crash
  • Embarazado: means pregnant as well as embarrassed
  • Fábrica: means factory
  • Injuria: refers to a moral or psychological pain such as an insult
  • Molestar: means to bother not molest
